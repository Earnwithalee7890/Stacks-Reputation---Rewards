# 🚀 Builder Quick Start Guide

Welcome to EarnWithAlee! Use this guide to start building your on-chain reputation.

## 1. Connect Your Wallet
- Use **Leather** or **Xverse** browser extension.
- Ensure you have some STX for gas (though core actions are currently free!).

## 2. Register Your Identity
- Go to the **Dashboard**.
- Enter your GitHub handle.
- This creates a permanent link between your code contributions and your Stacks address.

## 3. Daily Heartbeat
- Check in once every 24 hours.
- Building a streak is the fastest way to earn the **Master Builder SBT**.

## 4. Earn Reputation Badges
- **Novice**: 1 check-in
- **Builder**: 7-day streak
- **Architect**: Verified by a project
- **Master**: Community-recognized through bounties

## 5. Participate in Bounties
- Look for open-source tasks in the **Bounty Board**.
- Submit your work and get rewarded in STX tokens directly to your wallet.

Join us on [Stacks Discord](https://discord.gg/stacks) if you have any questions!
